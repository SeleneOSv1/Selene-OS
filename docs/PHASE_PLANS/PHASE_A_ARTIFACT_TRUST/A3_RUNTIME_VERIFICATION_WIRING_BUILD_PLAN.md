**C) CANONICAL A3 WIRING DESIGN**
1. Ingress accepts only session/turn context, artifact references already present in the request/runtime path, receipt refs, attestation refs, device-turn ordering, and platform context inputs. It does not accept client-asserted verification outcomes, trust roots, or final trust decisions as authoritative.
2. The adapter may normalize only platform/device posture: integrity status, compatibility status, negotiated capabilities, attestation presence, capture timestamps, receipt presence, and operational client metadata. Its current capture-artifact field becomes explicitly non-authoritative platform posture.
3. PH1.OS may normalize only orchestration legality and operational posture: wake-trigger legality, receipt/attestation completeness, capture retention checks, device-sync worker timing, and upstream duplex constraints. PH1.OS must not verify digests, signatures, trust roots, certification, revocation, lineage, or scope.
4. Section 04 receives the canonical `RuntimeExecutionEnvelope` plus carried artifact refs and normalized upstream posture. Section 04 is the only place where first-time authoritative artifact trust verification happens.
5. `ArtifactTrustDecisionRecord` is minted inside Section 04 immediately after authoritative verification succeeds or fails. It binds the carried artifact ref, trust binding ref, trust policy snapshot, trust set snapshot, verification result, basis fingerprint, provenance, and any future consumer hints defined by A2.
6. `artifact_trust_state` is attached to the envelope immediately after Section 04 mints the decision records. The envelope becomes the only transport for authoritative artifact trust state in runtime. Any derived compatibility view must be derived from `decision_records` only and remain non-authoritative.
7. Downstream components may read only. Executors, builder/learn/selfheal/simcat consumers, and later PH1.J/GOV/LAW stages may inspect `artifact_trust_state` and `ArtifactTrustDecisionRecord`, but they may not recompute or reinterpret first-time trust locally.
8. Existing fields that must be deprecated or reclassified in A3: `capture_artifact_trust_verified` becomes platform capture posture only; device-sync hash/apply fields become operational transport/apply integrity only; `artifact_hash_sha256` and `signature_ref` remain temporary consumer-era fields only and must not become the new transport of truth.
9. What must explicitly NOT be wired yet: PH1.J proof entry emission, PH1.GOV behavior changes, PH1.LAW behavior changes, proof persistence, law escalation mapping, cache invalidation behavior, revocation enforcement behavior, storage migrations, and any executor-local trust fallback.

**D) WIRING OWNERSHIP MODEL**
- Section 03 ingress owns request acceptance, artifact-ref carriage, idempotency, and envelope creation only.
- The adapter owns normalization of device/platform/receipt/attestation posture only.
- PH1.OS owns orchestration legality and upstream posture normalization only.
- Section 04 authority owns the only first-time authoritative artifact verification step and mints `ArtifactTrustDecisionRecord`.
- Envelope/state propagation owns only transport of `artifact_trust_state` across the runtime; it does not reinterpret it.
- Executors own read-only consumption only.
- Future PH1.J, PH1.GOV, and PH1.LAW stages own downstream consumption only, not trust recomputation.
